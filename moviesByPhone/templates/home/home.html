Recs By Text

Creating an Account

Creating an account is easy. Just text 'I am ' followed by your whatever you would like your username to be:

1-(301)-300-3110

Usernames must be one word.

I am karl

karl successfully created

Adding to your own list.

Text 'rec YOUR REC HERE to me' to add a movie to your own list.

rec Ghostbusters to me

Ghostbusters was added to your list. Reply to add context.

Adding Context

You can add some extra info about why you are making this recommendation. Simply reply to add context.

Ghostbusters was added to your list. Reply to add context.

Was talking about horror comedies with Karl

Context added

View Your List

You can check your list at any time by texting 'list'.

list

Movies
---------------------
1. Ghostbusters
2. Fargo
3. Widows

View A Single Recommendation

You can text 'show' plus the number of the movie in your list to see more info including links to imdb and Rotten tomatoes.

Movies
---------------------
1. Ghostbusters
2. Fargo
3. Widows

show 1

Ghostbusters
---------------------
CONTEXT: Was talking about horror comedies with Karl
IMDB: shritco.de/KRmZk
RT: shritco.de/KRmZk

Delete

You can delete recommendations from your list by texting 'delete' plus the number of the recommendation.

delete 1

Ghostbusters was deleted from your list.

Making a Recommendation

Sending a Recommendation to another user is easy. All you need is their username. 'rec MOVIENAME to USERNAME'

rec Princess Bride to karl123

Princess Bride was recommended to karl123. Reply to add context.

RE our convo about comedies that follow the hero's journey

Context added

Receiving a Recommendation

Only trusted users can add to your list. The first time a user makes a recommendation to your list, you will be asked if you want to add them as a trusted user. Once you do, any recommendations they make in the future will be automatically added. To add a new user as trusted, you'll need to reply with a unique identifier that will always start with 'r' and be followed by a number.

josh456 recommended 'Princess Bride' to you. Text back 'r55' if you would like to add this recommendation and them as a trusted user.

r55
