Recs By Text

Recommend movies to your friends and ask them for recommendations all through a convenient SMS interface.

Getting Started

To start a new account, text 'I am ' plus your username to 219-501-4076. Usernames cannot contain spaces.

Example: I am karl

Commands

List - Will return your list of recommended movies. When you first sign up, this list will be empty.

rec Ghostbusters to me - Will add a movie to your own list.

rec True Grit to josh - Will add a movie to your friend josh's list.

Show + the items position in the list to see more details about an item in your list

delete + the item's position in the list to delete the recommendation.
